Company Overview

Stasoul Nigeria Limited, founded in 2001 and officially commencing full operations in 2002, initially focused on general contracts. However, in May 2005, the company transitioned into the realm of Forensic Science, Security, and relatedbusiness services, marking a significant shift in our journey.

Our steadfast dedication to excellence is exemplified by the esteemed brands that have entrusted us with authorized and exclusive distributorships in the field of forensic science and security services, along with equipment supplies.
Furthermore, we have cultivated partnerships with established, certified, and globally experienced foreign companies, which have significantly contributed to creating a safer global community. These companies are renowned names in the realm of forensic science and security equipment manufacturing.

Stasoul Nigeria Limited, operating under RC NO 411575, is deeply engaged in providing products, services, and expertise within the domains of forensic, security, law enforcement, and business intelligence markets. Our directors and technical partners are recognized internationally for their unwavering commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction.

Our team comprises highly competent individuals, selected based on their innovative mindset and their relentless pursuit of professional growth.

Corporate Srategy

Stasoul’s operates on the premise of providing high quality services and products that exceeds customers and clients expectations. The company uses its team work to service local needs for its clients and ensure that our products and services meet the need of our clients locally as well as internationally.

Management Team

Our Management Team at Stasoul comprises seasoned professionals whose innate abilities have played a pivotal role in the substantial development and growth of our company. The individuals in our management staff have excelled in various fields, bringing their expertise to our operations:

  • OLASUPO UMAR BALOGUN – MD/CEO: Holding an OND in accounting from the Federal Polytechnic, Ado Ekiti in 1992, he serves as a director at Stasoul Nigeria Ltd. He was instrumental in the company's transformation, steering it from a trading entity to a prominent forensic/security services provider. His innate abilities, collaborative spirit, and innovative thinking have been instrumental in this evolution, and he has also pursued training in forensics both locally and internationally.

  • LATEEF UMAR BALOGUN – EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR FIN/ADMIN: Graduating from Rufus Giwa Polytechnic, Owo, Ondo State with an HND in Accounting in 1998, he brings his wealth of experience in financial accounting, complemented by strong managerial and administrative skills, to the valuable benefit of Stasoul Nigeria Ltd.

  • FOLA AIYEGBUSI – HEAD, MARKETING AND CORPORATE STRATEGY: Leading our marketing initiatives and strategies, Fola, one of the pioneer members of the Stasoul team, draws on over 18 years of extensive business experience, including consulting, technical expertise, and leadership roles. His background in the development, marketing, and sales of advanced forensic science equipment and security-related services, coupled with a BA Hons in Philosophy from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University in 1990, positions him as a significant asset to our team.

  • FEMI OJO – HEAD, TECHNICAL TEAM: Responsible for the management of the technical team, Femi holds an HND in Computer Science from the Federal Polytechnic Ado Ekiti in 2005.

  • FATAI BABATUNDE AJILEYE – BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT MANAGER: Tasked with overseeing the overall business and strategy initiatives of the company, Fatai leverages over 5 years of experience in business management. He graduated from the Lagos State University with a degree in Business Administration and completed a master's in business administration from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University in Ile-Ife.
